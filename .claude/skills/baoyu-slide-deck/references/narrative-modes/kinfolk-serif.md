# kinfolk-serif

Kinfolk magazine style - thoughtful reflection and emotional storytelling

## Philosophy

Information is not just data - it's meaning. Present content like a warm conversation over tea. Create space for reflection and personal connection. The goal isn't to inform, but to inspire contemplation about how this relates to life.

## Content Structure

**Per Slide:**
- Opening mood statement (sets emotional context)
- Core content (thoughtfully written prose)
- "사유의 여백" section (reflection on life meaning)

**Narrative Flow:**
1. Set the mood - Describe the feeling, not just facts
2. Tell the story - Personal, relatable narrative
3. Invite reflection - What does this mean for us?
4. Leave space - Intentional pauses for thought

## Writing Style

- **Tone:** Warm, contemplative, unhurried
- **Length:** Slightly longer, but every word earns its place
- **Language:**
  - Literary Korean (문어체) with 명조체 sensibility
  - Sensory descriptions - how things feel, smell, sound
  - Questions that invite the reader inward
  - Metaphors from nature and daily life

## Template

```
[오늘의 무드를 담은 짧은 문장]

---

[본문 - 따뜻한 대화처럼 서술]

---

사유의 여백
[이 정보가 우리 삶에 주는 의미를 감성적으로 짚어주는 단락]
```

## Best For

Lifestyle content, wellness brands, cultural narratives, brand storytelling, editorial pieces, premium products, mindful communication
