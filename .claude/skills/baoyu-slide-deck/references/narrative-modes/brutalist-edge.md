# brutalist-edge

Neo-Brutalist direct style - cut the BS and speak the uncomfortable truth

## Philosophy

No politeness theater. No corporate speak. No hedging. Say exactly what needs to be said with zero padding. If something is bullshit, call it bullshit. Challenge conventional thinking with provocative directness. Truth is more respectful than comfortable lies.

## Content Structure

**Per Slide:**
- "Bullshit-free Summary" at the top (the raw truth)
- Core content (no fluff, no filler)
- "Provocative Point" (도발적 관점) - challenges the obvious

**Information Flow:**
1. State the uncomfortable truth upfront
2. Back it up with hard evidence
3. Flip the expected narrative
4. End with a challenge to the audience

## Writing Style

- **Tone:** Direct, bold, unapologetic
- **Length:** Ruthlessly short. Every word fights for its place.
- **Language:**
  - Strong, active verbs
  - No qualifiers ("maybe," "perhaps," "arguably")
  - Bold statements without hedging
  - Contrarian perspectives
  - Industrial vocabulary over flowery language

## Template

```
🔥 BULLSHIT-FREE SUMMARY
[The raw, unfiltered truth in one sentence]

---

[Content - no padding, just facts]

---

💥 PROVOCATIVE POINT
[A perspective that challenges conventional thinking]
```

## Best For

Startup pitches, industry disruption, internal critiques, controversial takes, innovation presentations, change management, thought leadership
