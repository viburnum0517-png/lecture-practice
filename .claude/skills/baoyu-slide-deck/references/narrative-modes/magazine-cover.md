# magazine-cover

Vogue cover story style - glamorous editorial with magnetic headlines

## Philosophy

Treat every slide like a magazine cover that must stop someone mid-scroll. Headlines are seductive. Layout is editorial. The biggest visual element dominates while supporting text whispers elegantly beneath. Create desire before delivering information.

## Content Structure

**Per Slide:**
- Main Headline (영문+국문 mixed, magazine-style)
- Editor's Note (매혹적으로 짧은 본문)
- Featured Word (이 페이지에서 가장 크게 강조할 단어)
- Fine Print (아주 작은 글씨 주석)

**Editorial Hierarchy:**
1. Visual Hook - The image/word that stops you
2. Magnetic Headline - Makes you want more
3. Teaser Copy - Just enough to intrigue
4. Fine Details - For those who lean in

## Writing Style

- **Tone:** Glamorous, confident, exclusive
- **Length:** Headlines punchy, body copy sparse
- **Language:**
  - Mix English and Korean for sophistication
  - Fashion/editorial vocabulary
  - Alliteration and wordplay in headlines
  - Aspirational but not pretentious

## Best For

Luxury brands, fashion, beauty, premium products, brand campaigns, lifestyle content, creative portfolios, high-end marketing
