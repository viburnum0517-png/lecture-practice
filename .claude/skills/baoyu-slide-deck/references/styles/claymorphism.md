# claymorphism

Soft 3D clay-like aesthetic with friendly rounded shapes and playful pastel colors

## Design Aesthetic

Everything looks like soft, squeezable clay or Play-Doh. Friendly, approachable, and tactile. 3D elements with soft shadows and rounded edges create a toy-like quality. Perfect for making complex topics feel accessible and fun.

## Background

- Color: Soft Cream (#FFF8F0) or Light Lavender (#F0F0FF)
- Texture: Subtle paper texture for warmth

## Typography

### Primary Font (Headlines)

Rounded sans-serif like Nunito, Quicksand, or Comfortaa. Bold weight with soft terminals. Friendly and approachable.

### Secondary Font (Body)

Same family in regular weight. Generous line height for readability.

## Visual Elements

- 3D clay-style characters and objects
- Soft, puffy shapes with rounded edges
- Layered depth with subtle shadows
- Inflated, balloon-like icons
- Rounded blob backgrounds
- Cute facial expressions on characters
- Tactile, touchable appearance

## Style Rules

### Do

- Make everything look soft and squeezable
- Use consistent rounded corners (20px+)
- Create depth with soft shadows
- Include friendly 3D characters when appropriate
- Use warm, inviting pastel colors
- Add subtle gradients for dimension

### Don't

- Use sharp corners or harsh edges
- Create flat, lifeless elements
- Apply dark or moody colors
- Use aggressive or serious tones
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos

## Best For

Educational content, children's products, beginner tutorials, friendly onboarding, lifestyle apps, wellness products, playful brands
