# hand-drawn

Authentic pen-on-paper sketch aesthetic with organic imperfections and human warmth

## Design Aesthetic

Looks like someone drew it by hand with a pen or marker. Organic, imperfect lines create authenticity and warmth. Perfect for brainstorming visuals, creative processes, and making complex ideas feel approachable and personal.

## Background

- Color: Warm Off-White (#FFFEF5) or Kraft Paper (#D4C4A8)
- Texture: Paper grain texture, notebook lines optional

## Typography

### Primary Font (Headlines)

Hand-drawn style fonts like Caveat, Permanent Marker, or Architects Daughter. Bold strokes for emphasis.

### Secondary Font (Body)

Same family or complementary handwriting font. Natural variation in letterforms.

## Visual Elements

- Hand-drawn arrows and connectors
- Doodle-style icons and symbols
- Scribbled underlines and circles
- Sticky note style callouts
- Thought bubbles and speech bubbles
- Checkboxes and bullet points
- Messy but intentional annotations
- Lightbulb and star icons

## Style Rules

### Do

- Embrace imperfection in line work
- Use varied line weights
- Add annotations and margin notes
- Include sketch-style icons
- Create organic, flowing connections
- Mix different "pen" colors purposefully

### Don't

- Make lines too perfect or mechanical
- Use photorealistic elements
- Over-polish the aesthetic
- Add too many colors at once
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos

## Best For

Brainstorming sessions, creative pitches, educational content, startup ideas, workshop materials, informal presentations, design thinking
